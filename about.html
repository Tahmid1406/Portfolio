A brief biography | My Story

Being born among the hills and sea, I was raised among the richness of mother nature. I'm talking about the beautiful port city of Bangladesh named Chottogram, where I was born in the year 1998. My father was a government employee and being transferred to new places was a part of our life. Although with every transfer I had to leave my school, teachers and friends behind, but, I was presented with the opportunity to meet new people, be among a different cultures, adapt to different localities and so on.

After finishing my early schooling by the year 2012, I took science as my group of study (based on Bangladesh education system). I was always amazed to see how there's science in everything, from a ball point to a space station, from above and beyond! I passed both the Junior (2012) and Secondary (2013) education board exams (publicly held in Bangladesh) with perfect scores of 5.00/5.00 with "talent-pool scholarships" in both of them which was awarded by the Education Ministry of Bangladesh. In the higher secondary education board exams, I again gained a perfect score of 5.00/5.00 . After passing the HSC, without any second thought, I choose Computer Science & Engineering as my subject for higher studies. I still remember the day when the first computer arrived at my house. It had a pentium processor CPU with 64 MB of RAM, CD writter, floppy disk for sharing files and a CRT monitor. It was an amazing journey of mine to see computers grow this powerful from those of computers. With great passion, I got admitted to the Electrical and Computer Engineering department of North South University, Dhaka which is among the top ranked (among top 5) universities in Bangladesh.

University is the place where I met with brilliant minds and passionate people. The four years of my university life have had a great impact on me becoming the person I am today. I had the amazing opportunity to work with people like AKM Bahalul Haque sir for my junior design project (CSE299) and directed research (CSE 498R) where I got introduced to academic research. In my final year, I was lucky enough to get Dr. Mohammad Rashedur Rahman sir as my supervisor for Senior Design Project (CSE499). The 8 month long design project ended with two peer-reviewed papers. After graduation, I started working as an Research Assistant under Dr. Rashed and our project has been funded by North South University.

I believe it's essential for every person to do what he loves and to love what he does. So, Finding out a proper purpose to wake up every morning is important to me. Otherwise, it becomes difficult to get things done! I am one of those blessed people who found their purpose of life. As Mr. Elon Musk said, "When something is important enough, you do it even if the odds are not in your favor", I truly believe that hardwork and dedication works against all the odds. This is what motivates me and keeps me going.
